ABOUT US
Code-N-Build is a one-of-a-kind hackathon cum technical extravaganza with a fiesta of events, competitions, quizzes, practice tracks, speaker sessions, and the amazing CNB 2.0 hackathon. We intend to encourage healthy competition to incubate futuristic ideas. Code-N-Build focuses on providing students and entrepreneurs with a platform to develop, compete, network and present their ideas to solve real-world problems. Initiated back in 2019 at JECRC University this event was a huge success and gained national recognition

Again hosted by JECRC University, Jaipur and this time organised by GOOGLE- Developer Student Clubs, the CODE-N-BUILD 2.0 is all set to surpass all it’s previous records and benchmarks and build a nexus of global developers within India. We aim to guide, nurture and mentor young and innovative minds in the field of Computer Science and Technology and provide them ample resources to make them capable of everything they wish to achieve.

Education

Formulate new ways to lessen the boundaries and bridge the gap in learning. Come up with solutions to the challenges which still exist in the education system.In the past few months, the ways of education have evolved a lot due to the pandemic and thus the sector of online education requires a lot of contributions. Innovate something which is capable of making each student tech-efficient and is beneficial to them.

Rural Revolution

It’s time that we focus on Technology Solutions that can ENHANCE life in rural India by diffusing critical problems they face.. Let's move ahead from REFLECTION TO ACTION. You can turn your innovative ideas into action for making a social change. This is your chance to participate and build your apps and solutions that will uplift the lives of fellow citizens in rural and impoverished areas, who have limited access to technology, communication, and knowledge.

Health-Tech

You all are aware of the critical times we are in. We need to frame new apps and innovative projects to make our health sector better and try our best to serve the whole world. You all have the power and knowledge of technology and “with great powers, comes great responsibility.” We as developers can serve humankind with the use of our programs. You will not only win this hackathon but also the hearts of billions of people! Let’s use our minds and codes to make this earth a healthier place!

Open-Innovation

Open innovation is “the use of purposive inflows and outflows of knowledge to accelerate internal innovation, and expand the markets for external use of innovation, respectively.”It is the antithesis of the traditional vertical integration approach where internal R&D activities lead to internally developed products that are distributed by the firm. It provides a deep insight into the needs of the customers and contributors. So open up your mind and build something out of the box in this track!

Fin-Tech

Fintech, the abbreviation for financial technology, is a broad category that refers to the innovative use of technology in the design and delivery of financial services and products. The application of fintech cuts across multiple business segments, including lending, advice, investment management and payments. So let’s just innovate something which will reshape and improve finance by cutting costs, extend financial inclusion, expanding access to financial services and improve the daily lives of people, and spur growth.
